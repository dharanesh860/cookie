Our Story

From comedy clubs to cookie jars

The Beginning

Founded by two comedians who shared a passion for baking, Stand-Up Cookie was born from the idea that life is too short for boring cookies. Our journey began in a small kitchen, where laughter and flour filled the air.

Our Mission

We believe that every cookie should bring joy and laughter to your day. Our mission is to create premium cookies that not only taste amazing but also bring a smile to your face with every bite.

The Process

Each cookie is crafted with premium ingredients and a dash of humor, making every treat a memorable experience. From our classic Chocolate Chip Comedian to the unpredictable Oatmeal Improv, we guarantee a performance that will leave you wanting an encore!
